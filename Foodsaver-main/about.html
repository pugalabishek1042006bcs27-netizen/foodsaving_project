About FoodSavr

Our Mission

FoodSavr is dedicated to reducing food waste while addressing food insecurity. We connect food donors with receivers through volunteers, ensuring surplus food reaches those in need.

📊 Impact Statistics

  • • 50,000+ meals rescued
  • • 200+ active volunteers
  • • 150+ partner organizations
  • • 25 tons of food waste prevented

🌱 Environmental Benefits

  • • Reduced landfill waste
  • • Lower carbon emissions
  • • Sustainable food system
  • • Community awareness
